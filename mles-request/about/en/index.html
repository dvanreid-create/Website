What you get

Tell us an act you're considering and 2–3 possible dates. We email you your MLE Score within 24h: the score, the reasons in plain language, a date comparison (which draws best), and a recommendation.

How we read demand

Your MLE Score is produced by a proprietary, self-learning model built specifically for this market. Its architecture is grounded in established econometric and regression methods, calibrated against demand patterns observed across the Málaga city and Costa del Sol region. The model evaluates three families of variables, then applies adjustments for conditions such as one-off scarcity or open-air weather risk:

The actDrawing power and regional relevance, benchmarked against comparable acts.
This audienceEstimated fit with the coast's distinctive resident-and-tourist demographic mix.
The setupVenue capacity and prestige, timing, and price positioning.

The Costa del Sol is an atypical market — a large British and Nordic base, both resident and tourist, layered over the local audience. Because the model is fitted to these regional demand elasticities rather than national averages, an act with strong local pull can be projected to out-draw a larger global name whose core audience simply isn't on the coast.

A "glass box"

What it's not

A directional demand estimate, not a guarantee of attendance or ticket revenue. It's there to sharpen a booking decision, not replace your judgement.
